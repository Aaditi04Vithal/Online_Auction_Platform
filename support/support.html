Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Login

  • How do I create an account?

    Click on the "Sign Up" button on the top right of the homepage and fill in the details.

  • I forgot my password. What should I do?

    Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions.

  • Can I change my email after registration?

    Yes, go to Account Settings to update your email address.

Ordering & Payment

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and Google Pay.

  • How can I track my order?

    You will receive a confirmation email with a tracking link, or you can check "My Orders".

  • Can I cancel or change my order?

    Orders can be canceled or modified within 24 hours by contacting support.

Shipping & Delivery

  • How long does shipping take?

    Shipping takes 3-5 business days. Delivery options will appear during checkout.

  • Do you offer international shipping?

    Yes, we ship to select countries. Shipping costs will vary.

  • How do I return a product?

    Contact us within 30 days to initiate a return.

Technical Support

  • The website isn't loading correctly. What should I do?

    Clear your cache, try a different browser, or contact technical support.

  • I'm having trouble with the mobile app. Who can help?

    Contact our app support team via email at appsupport@yourwebsite.com.
